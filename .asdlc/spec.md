# Overview

An internal leave management application where WSO2 employees request time off and managers approve or deny those requests.

# Personas

- Employee — requests leave and checks their balance
- Manager — reviews and approves leave requests from their team
- HR administrator — manages leave policies and balances

# Features

- Employees submit leave requests by selecting dates and choosing a leave type.
- Managers see a list of pending requests from their direct reports.
- Managers approve or reject requests with an optional comment.
- Employees view their current leave balances for different leave types.
- Everyone can see their own request history and approval status.
